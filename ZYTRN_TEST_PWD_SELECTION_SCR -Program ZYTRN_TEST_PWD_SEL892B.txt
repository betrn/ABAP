REPORT  zytrn_test_pwd_selection_scr  .
PARAMETERS: password(10) TYPE c.

INITIALIZATION.
  LOOP AT SCREEN.
    CHECK screen-name CS 'PASSWORD' and
          screen-input = 1.
    screen-invisible = 1.
    MODIFY SCREEN.
  ENDLOOP.

START-OF-SELECTION.
  WRITE: / password.
